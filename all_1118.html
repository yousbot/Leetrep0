1118. Number of Days in a Month

Given a year Y and a month M , return how many days there are in that month.

Example 1: 

Input: Y = 1992, M = 7
Output: 31

Example 2: 

Input: Y = 2000, M = 2
Output: 29

Example 3: 

Input: Y = 1900, M = 2
Output: 28

Note:

  1. 1583 <= Y <= 2100
  2. 1 <= M <= 12

Easy
