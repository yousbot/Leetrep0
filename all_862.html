862. Shortest Subarray with Sum at Least K

Return the length of the shortest, non-empty, contiguous subarray of A with sum at least K .

If there is no non-empty subarray with sum at least K , return -1 .

Example 1: 

Input: A = [1], K = 1
Output: 1

Example 2: 

Input: A = [1,2], K = 4
Output: -1

Example 3: 

Input: A = [2,-1,2], K = 3
Output: 3

Note:

  1. 1 <= A.length <= 50000
  2. -10 ^ 5 <= A[i] <= 10 ^ 5
  3. 1 <= K <= 10 ^ 9

Difficulty:

Hard

Lock:

Normal
